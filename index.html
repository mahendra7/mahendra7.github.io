About Me

I graduated from Syracuse University with a Masters Degree in Computer Engineering in 2008.

I worked at Cisco Systems from April 2013 until October 2016 as a Front-end Automation and Command Line/Feature Automation Engineer. I am fluent in Linux, Java, Python, JUnit, TestNG, Cucumber, WebDriver, Jenkins, Subversion and Git. I have worked on both a Software Defined Network solution called Extensible Network Controller (XNC) used to configure and monitor large networks and Unified Computing Systems (UCS), a high available data center solution.

I worked as a QA engineer from March 2011 until April 2013 at Google Inc in New York City testing a Video Conference System based on Google Talk. In just two years, the number of video conference rooms skyrocketed from 400 to 6000. At Google, my close-knit team of 14 engineers tested and verified the integration of Google Video Conference with other Google products such as Google Docs, Youtube, Google Calendar and Google Voice.

I am also well-versed in HTML5, CSS, Ruby on Rails, Javascript, NodeJS, ExpressJS, jQuery, MongoDB, MySQL, React, React Native, Redux, ES6, GraphQL, Webpack, Babel, NPM, Responsive Web Design, Heroku and AWS.
